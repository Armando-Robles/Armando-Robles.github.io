Welcome to our online farm!

We have been serving our community in the Western half of the United States for over 3 generations! We have three main attractions: our massive Vineyard, our Venue and most importantly our 420 Acre farm!!

We are a local, family owned and operated farm in the neighboring valley.

Here on the farm we grow our top rated corn, potatoes, carrots and tomatoes! We've recently partitioned a small portion of our farm to start a vineyard. We've also spent the past 3 years hosting venues for weddings and large events! We are a fourth generation farm, so now its all profit! Back in the ol' days we used to run 'shine to the ol' speekeasies! Since then we have gone clean to run a 100% legit farm! It aint much, but its honest work.
